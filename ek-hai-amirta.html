Aik he amirta

Modern Poets Presents EK Hai Amrita | Revenue Stamp of Free Soul A Theatrical Play Dedicated to Amrita Pritam On the occasion of Amrita`s 104th Birth Anniversary, we bring you a Poetic Musical, Dance & Storytelling show dedicated to Amrita`s life. Introduction of Amrita and her initial journey, Sahir in Amrita`s life Through Music, Storytelling and Act, Live Recital of Amrita and Imorz`s Letter and Act, A dance representation of Amrita`s Poetry.

Adapted & Directed By: Mohit Dwivedi Artist(s): Pallavi as Amrita Pritam Aksha as Young Amrita Anshul as Narrator, Storyteller and Nand Sadhu Bashir as Sahir Ludhianvi Anksh as Shiv Kumar Batalavi Smriti as Sara Shagufta Mohit as Imroz
